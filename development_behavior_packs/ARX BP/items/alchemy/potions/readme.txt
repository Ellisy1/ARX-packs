*_d_upgrade in filename - Duration upgrade 
*_p_upgrade in filename - Power upgrade

=== ARX POTIONS ===

+P = power upgrade: usually 2x more effective than original
+D = duration upgrade: (not always, but usually) for buffs: 8 mins vs 3 for non-upgraded, for debuffs: 2 mins vs 0.5 for non-upgraded
isA = is alternative craft to vanilla
